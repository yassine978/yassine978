## About Me

I'm an AI/ML engineer finishing my **M2 in Artificial Intelligence, Systems & Data** at **Université Paris Dauphine-PSL**, currently building production LLM systems at **Attijari Bank Tunisia's Innovation Team**.

My focus is **multi-agent orchestration, RAG architecture, and hybrid ML+LLM pipelines** for domains where explainability and auditability matter — banking, insurance, and regulated data environments. I care about systems that are honest about their own limitations: correctly-calibrated cache behavior, transparent fallback paths, and evaluation numbers that hold up under scrutiny.

**Currently:**
- 🏦 Building **CREDITAI**, a 13-agent LangGraph credit evaluation platform, at Attijari Bank Tunisia
- 🎓 Finishing my master's thesis and defense at Paris Dauphine-PSL
- 💼 Freelancing part-time on Django/automation projects via ScribLogik

**Open to:** full-time AI/ML engineering roles from mid-2026, and freelance RAG/LLM automation projects in the meantime.
